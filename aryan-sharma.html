01 — About

A small town,
a different path.

I grew up in Sarkaghat, a small town in Mandi district, Himachal Pradesh. After completing my BSc in Computer Science from DAV College, Chandigarh in 2023, I didn't take the usual route.

Instead of job hunting, I spent that time learning markets — studying price, structure, and how capital moves. Trading made sense to me in a way that a 9-to-5 didn't. So I went all in.

I've been active in student politics during college, which gave me a different kind of education — how to stay calm under pressure, think on your feet, and own your decisions.

Now I trade gold and crypto for prop firms. I work 24/5, manage funded accounts, and keep learning. I'm also interested in tech and plan to build on this foundation as I grow.

"I didn't come from a trading family or a finance background. I just found something I was genuinely interested in and stayed consistent with it."
